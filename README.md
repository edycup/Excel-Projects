### 1. Market Analysis (Pivot Tables)
**Task:** Analyze a dataset of global bike sales to determine market reach and profitability across different demographics .

### 2. Automated Categorization (Logic Functions)
**Task:** Categorize product sales in various English counties (Yorkshire, Cornwall, etc.) based on volume thresholds .
* **Logic Used:** Utilized the `SWITCH` function to label sales >600 as "High" and <300 as "Low" .
* **Formula:** `=SWITCH(TRUE, C2 > 600, "High", C2 >= 300, "Medium", "Low")`.
